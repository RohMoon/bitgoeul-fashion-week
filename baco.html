Baco92

baco92's philosophy culminates in the creation of a new, deconstructed RTW (Ready-To-Wear) by seamlessly blending tailoring, streetwear, and couture techniques. Beyond mere wearability, the core intention behind our designs is to stimulate contemporary society by presenting garments imbued with specific memories and concepts from the past. This approach not only allows the clothing itself to embody profound meaning but also aims to forge a more provocative and impactful modern apparel.
